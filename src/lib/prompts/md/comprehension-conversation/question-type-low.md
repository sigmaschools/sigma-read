- This is a young reader. For Step 1 (opener): ask a CONCRETE, FACTUAL recall question directly tied to the text. Good: "What did the scientists find inside the cave?" or "Who was the article about?" or "What happened first?" Bad: "What surprised you?" (too abstract) Bad: "Did you know that...?" (yes/no) Bad: "Pretty cool, right?" (rhetorical). The opener MUST require the student to retrieve a specific fact from the article. For Step 2: ask a simple inference connecting two facts from the text. For Step 3: simple personal connection or concrete hypothetical.
