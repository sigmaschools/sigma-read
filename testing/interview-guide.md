# SigmaRead UX Interview Guide

**Interviewer:** Cole
**Subject:** Wayne Vaughan (Founder)
**Date:** February 7, 2026
**Duration:** ~30 minutes

---

## Track 1: Raw UX Reactions (Guide Experience)

_Wayne tests the app as Calie (guide) and reports what he sees._

### First Impressions
1. You just logged in as a guide for the first time. What do you see? What's your immediate reaction?
2. What would you do first? Why?
3. Is anything confusing or unclear?

### Student Overview
4. Look at the student list. What information jumps out? What's missing?
5. Can you tell which students need your attention right now? How?
6. Pick a student who seems to be struggling. Walk me through what you see on their detail page.

### Comprehension Reports
7. Open a comprehension report. Does this tell you what you need to know?
8. What would you do with this information as a guide?
9. Is there anything here that would make you worried? Reassured?

### Student Detail Deep Dive
10. Look at the score trend chart. Is it useful? What would make it more useful?
11. Open a transcript. Does this give you signal? Or is it noise?

### Overall Guide Experience
12. If you had 15 students instead of 8, would this dashboard still work?
13. What's the one thing you'd want to see that isn't here?
14. Rate the guide experience 1-10. Why that number?

---

## Track 2: Product Vision & Boundaries

_Wayne speaks as founder, not tester._

### Core Identity
15. Complete this sentence: "SigmaRead is a _____ that helps _____ do _____."
16. What is SigmaRead NOT? (List 3-5 things people might assume it is.)
17. If SigmaRead does one thing perfectly, what is that one thing?

### The Core Loop
18. Walk me through the ideal student session — what does a perfect 15-minute session look like?
19. What's the minimum viable interaction? (The smallest unit of value.)
20. What breaks the experience? What would make a student not come back?

### Target Users
21. Describe the ideal guide. What's their day like? What do they need from SigmaRead?
22. Describe the ideal student. Age, reading level, motivation level.
23. Who is SigmaRead NOT for? (Be specific.)

### Scope & Direction
24. What features should we explicitly NOT build in the next 30 days?
25. What's the difference between SigmaRead and a chatbot that quizzes kids on articles?
26. Where does SigmaRead fit in the broader Sigma ecosystem? (Compass, Sigma Score, etc.)

### Success Metrics
27. How do you know SigmaRead is working? What would you measure?
28. What does a successful pilot with real students look like?
29. What would make you say "this isn't working, pivot"?

### Competitive Positioning
30. A parent asks "why not just use Khan Academy / Reading Eggs / ChatGPT?" — what's your answer?
